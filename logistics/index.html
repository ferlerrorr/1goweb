Logistics

1Go Logistics manage all your clients and 3PLs in one system. Gain real-time visibility and control of your entire Logistics operations , supply chain and software development.

In this day and age of fast paced and diverse technologies, the need for a consolidated, single-view and point of management become more imminent. As competition gets tighter and customer service become more demanding, organizations are now looking at business process improvements and operational efficiency to address these challenges.

Transport Management System (TMS)

Our TMS provides real-time operational visibility and control. Managers are equipped with a web-based portal that tracks all driver and shipment activities through a collaborative mobile delivery application. This system has route optimization capability that pre-plans and pre-schedules specific routes to maximize trips that equate to operational savings. Learn More

Control Tower

A system that coordinates orders and deliveries across the entire logistics distribution network. This is composed of a dispatch team, help desk, and shipment-costing personnel who analyze, assess, coordinate, and direct deliveries straight to the outlets.

Control Tower oversees and manages the process and movement of all distribution elements using web-enabled technologies for fast and efficient deliveries. Learn More
